The Particle System
by Stanley Hsieh and Joseph Collora
UWB CSS452 Winter 2023

Left Click: Spawn effect
Left/Right: To toggle between different effects

Fire Controls:
  • 1/2: Increase/Decrease Rate of Change (0.95-1.00)
  • 3/4: Increase/Decrease Fire Spread (0-10)
  • 5/6: Increase/Decrease Wind (-50-50)
  • 7/8: Increase/Decrease Height (50-100)
Smoke Controls:
  • Q/W:Increase/Decrease Thickness (1-5)
  • E/R: Increase/Decrease X Axis Coverage (10-50)
  • T/Y: Increase/Decrease Y Axis Coverage (10-50)
  • U/I: Increase/Decrease X Axis Wind (-25-25)
  • O/P: Increase/Decrease Y Axis Wind (-25-25)
Explosion Controls:
  • A/S: Increase/Decrease Radius (5-15)
  • D/F: Increase/Decrease Force (1-5)
